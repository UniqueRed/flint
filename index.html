First time opening flint

macOS may block apps downloaded outside the App Store. Here's how to allow it — takes about 30 seconds.

1

Open the DMG and drag flint into your Applications folder.

2

Try to open flint. macOS will show a warning saying it can't be opened. Click Done — this is expected.

3

Open System Settings and navigate to:

Privacy & Security›Security
4

Scroll down and you'll see a message saying "flint was blocked." Click Open Anyway, then confirm with your password or Touch ID.

5

Open flint from Applications. You're all set — you'll never need to do this again.

This prompt appears because flint is a free, open-source app distributed outside the App Store — not because anything is wrong with it. Apple requires a paid developer account to bypass this screen. Learn more about Gatekeeper ›
